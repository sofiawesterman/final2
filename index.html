The top movies of 2025- and what this tells us about the movie industry

By Sofia Westerman
Landmark Theatre, a crowd-favorite for Syracuse, NY residents

2025 started with a bang, with an array of movies released in the first four months of the year. Here is a breakdown of what you need to know.

Top Movie Distributors
This pie chart shows the top movie distrubtors of 2025

In the past four months, production giants have dominated the box office scene. Based on the top 20 movies in the box office, Warner Bros was the top distributor making a total of $549,236,152, correlating to 39.07% of the total box office. Warner Bros produced three movies in the top 20 list, with A Minecraft Movie at the #1 spot, Sinners at #3, and Mickey17 at #8.

Warner Bros employee Diego Aldana shares his take on the company’s success this quarter.When asked about their strong performance in the box office; “We’ve worked hard to create productions that appeal across demographics while leveraging new content in fresh, innovative ways.” Regarding their success with A Minecraft Movie, Aldana shares that it was largely due to the previous popularity of the game as well as marketing and partnerships. “Minecraft is one of the most recognizable gaming titles globally, with an enormous multigenerational fanbase. But success at this scale doesn’t happen from brand power alone — we also focused heavily on making the story accessible, visually engaging, and family-friendly.”

Coming in second grossing 22.16% of the top 20s box office is Mickey Mouse himself, Walt Disney Studios. Walt Disney Studios also produced 3 movies in the top 20 box office, with a total gross of $311,486,761. The studio produced the #2 pick Captain America: Brave New World, #5 Snow White, and Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith 20th anniversary placed in the 16th spot.

Most Popular Movies
This bar chart shows the top five movies of 2025

A Minecraft Movie topped the charts as the #1 spot on the domestic box office list. Released in April, this contributes greatly to April leading the way to the highest grossing Month of 2025 so far. A Minecraft Movie was advertised as a comedic action adventure for families. At the movie’s opening on April 4th, it made $162,753,003, screening in 4,263 theaters in the US. The movie grossed a domestic total of $380,953,914 thus far.

Falling in second place, but not close behind was Captain America: Brave New World . This movie grossed $200,224,789 domestically, making $88,842,603 on its February 14th release, premiering in 4,105 theaters. The movie’s lower revenue could be due to its PG-13 rating, making this action-adventure flick less suitable for all audiences to enjoy.

But, I’d expect A Minecraft Movie to produce much more revenue in the near future, as it was released almost two months after Captain America: Brave New World, so it has not reached its full potential as the latter has.

Monthly Impacts
This line chart shows the amount of money made by the top movies in the boc office per month in 2025
This chart shows how many top movies were made in each month of 2025

Out of the four months we’ve experienced in 2025, April led the way as the top grossing month, with a total gross of $734,536,836. Popular movies such as The King of Kings, The Amatuer and The Accountant 2 were released in April, attributing to the month’s success. The primary successor of April was A Minecraft Movie, the highest grossing movie of 2025 so far, despite being released in the most recent month.

Although April had an almost $300,000 lead, February came in second place grossing $409,268,683. This is partially due to Captain America: Brave New World being released in February, as the second highest grossing movie of the 2025 box office. Other movies released in February include Paddington in Peru and The Monkey.

April has proved to have the highest amount of revenue in 2025, but it was not the biggest month for movie production. Out of 146 top box office productions, March ended with 41 movies, with April and February following in suit with 36, and January falling short with 33. The post-holiday slump must affect the movie industry as well!

2025 has paved the way to be a great year for the box office, and May shows great promise, with anticipated movies Mission Impossible- The Final Reckoning, Thunderbolts and Final Destination premiering in the next month.
